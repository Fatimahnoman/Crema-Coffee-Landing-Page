A small room, a slow ritual

Crema started with one second-hand espresso machine and a stubborn idea: that a cup made with care is worth waiting for. We roast in-house every Tuesday, pull each shot by hand, and bake the morning's pastries before the sun is properly up.

No rush, no syrupy shortcuts. Just good beans, real milk, and a corner of the old town that feels like yours.

Mara & Idris, founders
